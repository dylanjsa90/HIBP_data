The background and purpose of the data is documented in this blog post: https://www.troyhunt.com/heres-1-4-billion-records-from-have-i-been-pwned-for-you-to-analyse

Each row contains a unique set of pwned websites delimited by a semicolon and followed by a count indicating how many unique email addresses these sites appear against. For example:

Adobe;Aipai;CivilOnline;NetEase;NexusMods 20

There are 20 unique email addresses that appear in this exact combination of 5 data breaches.

The data is an aggregate of 1,431,112,732 records being the total number of email addresses in HIBP as of 6 December 2016, EXCLUDING those that only appear in a "sensitive" data breach. Full details in the blog post above.
